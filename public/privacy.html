WHAN — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 3, 2026

Milakhin Studio ("we", "us") operates the WHAN mobile application.

Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use WHAN:

  • Email address (for account creation)
  • Username (chosen by you)
  • Game statistics (wins, losses, MMR rating)
  • Game history

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the game service
  • To display leaderboards and player profiles
  • To improve the game experience

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — database and authentication
  • Google Sign In — optional authentication
  • Apple Sign In — optional authentication

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on Supabase servers. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Children's Privacy

WHAN is rated 4+ and does not knowingly collect data from children under 13 without parental consent.
