Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start a game?

Go to "Rooms" tab, tap "Create" to create a new room, add bots or invite friends, then tap "Ready".

What is 4♠ (4 of Spades)?

The player holding 4♠ always goes first. Playing it as your last card against one opponent is called "Coronation" and earns bonus points!

How does MMR work?

Win games to earn MMR points. Every 3 consecutive wins earns a streak bonus. Your rank increases as you earn more MMR.

How do I invite friends?

Create a room, tap the "Link" button in the waiting room, and share the invite link with friends.

What are Scheduled Games?

You can plan a game in advance. Go to "Create", select "Schedule 2x2", pick date and time. Friends can accept and you'll get reminders 15 min before.
